FACTS ABOUT BIRYANI

Biryani is a fragrant mixed rice dish with ancient roots, named from the Persian 'biryan' (fried before cooking), popularized in India by the Mughals.

Studying biryani reveals a rich history, blending Persian influences (like layered cooking and saffron) with indegenious Indian spicy rice dishes.
